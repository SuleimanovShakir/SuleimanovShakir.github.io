I'm a [RESPIRE-EXCEL](https://www.respire-excel.eu) MSCA Doctoral Network PhD student at UMCG, working with [Martijn Nawijn](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=2c19wW4AAAAJ&hl=en) (UMCG) and [Malte Lücken](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Nr4KBvUAAAAJ&hl=en) (Helmholtz Munich) on integrating single-cell and single-nucleus data into reference atlases for chronic inflammatory airway diseases — currently the Human Lung Cell Atlas v2 and a COPD-airways atlas.

I also teach R, statistics, and machine learning at the [Bioinformatics Institute](https://bioinf.institute) and mentor early-career researchers through [Sci.STEPS](https://www.scisteps.org).
